Indian Curried Chickpeas

Ingredients

1 onion, chopped
2 tbsp olive oil
1/2 cup vegetable broth
2 15+1/2 ounce cans chickpeas (garbanzo beans), drained
1 tsp curry powder
1 tsp coriander powder or turmeric
1 tsp cumin
3 tbsp prepared chutney
2 15+1/2 ounce cans diced tomatoes (no need to drain)

Instructions

First, heat the olive oil over medium heat in a large skillet or sauce pan.
Add the chopped onion and sautee the onion just until soft, about 3 to 5 minutes.
Add remaining ingredients and stir to combine well. 
Cover, and allow to cook for at least 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until heated through.
